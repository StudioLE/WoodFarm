Wood Farm is home to Felthorpe Airfield, formerly the Felthorpe Flying Group. The airfield has over 50 years of aviation history and is now home to over 20 aircraft.

More information about the airfield and its history can be found on the volunteer run website.

Enquiries can be made by email to hello@woodfarm.org.
