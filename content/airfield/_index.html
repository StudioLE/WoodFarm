The airfield at Wood Farm is home to and operated by Felthorpe Flying Group. The club has a long aviation history and is now home to around 20 aircraft, two hangers and the club house.

More information about the club and their aircraft can be found on the Felthorpe Flying Group website.

Enquiries can be made by email to hello@woodfarm.org.
